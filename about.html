About Me

Welcome! I'm Darshan Trivedi, a financial analyst based in Halifax, Canada, with a passion for investment research, equity analysis, and macroeconomic trends.

Professional Background

I currently work at Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) with experience in portfolio management, financial instrument reconciliation, and investment analysis. My career has focused on middle office operations, portfolio valuation, P&L reporting, and client relationship management.

Previously, I worked at Citco, developing expertise in financial instruments, derivatives, and portfolio operations.

CFA Journey

I am a CFA Level 3 candidate, currently pursuing the Private Wealth Pathway. I completed CFA Level 2 and am committed to continuous learning and professional development in the field of investment management.

Areas of Expertise

My areas of focus include: Equity research and analysis, Portfolio management and valuation, Macroeconomic analysis, Commodity markets (gold, silver), ETF and index fund analysis, Indian fiscal policy and budgets, Financial modeling and ratio analysis.
